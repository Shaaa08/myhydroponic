Hydroponic Kit

A hydroponic kit includes essentials like growing containers, nutrient solution, air pumps, and grow lights. These kits are designed to help you set up your own hydroponic system at home or in a small-scale setup, ensuring that you have all the tools necessary to grow plants without soil.
