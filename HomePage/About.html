About MyHydroponic

Welcome to MyHydroponic, your ultimate solution for growing plants using hydroponics! Our platform helps you learn how to manage and grow your hydroponic garden, offers useful resources on plant care, and even allows you to monitor the health of your plants using advanced image recognition.

We are dedicated to making hydroponic gardening accessible and enjoyable for everyone, whether you're a beginner or an experienced gardener.
